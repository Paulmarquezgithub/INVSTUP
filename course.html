Certification Overview

INVST Advanced Finance and Investment Certification is structured to provide a deep dive into the realms of corporate finance, investment strategies, and financial analysis.

  • Entirely free, allowing accessibility to all aspiring finance professionals or enthusiasts.
  • Upon successful completion, display this certification on LinkedIn, enhancing your professional visibility and credibility.

Course Structure

Our certification program is segmented into three levels, each designed to build upon the last, ensuring a thorough understanding and application of financial principles:

Course 1: Foundations of Investment and Finance

  • Introduction to Financial Concepts: Basic principles, time value of money, and financial statement analysis.
  • Asset Classes: Deep dive into stocks, bonds, commodities, and real estate.
  • Introduction to Portfolio Theory: Basics of risk management and diversification.

Course 2: Intermediate Financial Analysis and Investment Strategies

  • Corporate Finance: Capital structure decisions, dividend policy, and mergers & acquisitions.
  • Advanced Portfolio Management: Modern portfolio theory, CAPM, and performance metrics like Sharpe Ratio.
  • Fixed Income and Derivatives: Understanding bonds, options, and futures.

Course 3: Advanced Investment Techniques and Financial Strategies

  • Behavioral Finance: Impact of psychology on financial markets and investment decisions.
  • Quantitative Finance: Use of algorithms in trading and advanced financial modeling.
  • Ethical Investing: ESG factors, impact investing, and their influence on portfolio construction.
